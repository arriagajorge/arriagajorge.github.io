Data Scientist Researcher

Instituto Nacional de Medicina Genómica | Feb 2023- Jul 2023

In my primary role, I focused on identifying overexpressed genes in lung cancer tissues. To achieve this, I meticulously analyzed TCGA data, which was thoroughly preprocessed and normalized. Employing sparse general canonical analysis and mutual information, I constructed networks to serve as potential regulatory models. Stochastic enumeration method for counting trees (BSc Mathematics thesis)

Open Source/Spanish

The main objective is to present tree counting methods, focusing especially on the analysis of the Stochastic Enumeration Method, and to demonstrate that this method produces a significant reduction in variance compared to the Knuth estimator. In addition, we will show how the enumeration method works and explore possible practical applications of this approach e.g. network reliability and counting SAT.
